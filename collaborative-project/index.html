About

Our School

We are a community of artists, makers, students and teachers who are committed to the preservation of paper carving craft. In our San Francisco studio we partner with local artists and crafts people to offer a wide variety of classes on different styles of paper carving. From sophisticated oriental influenced styles to simple and fun paper cut-outs for any occasion.
